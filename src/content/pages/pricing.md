We keep pricing simple and scope work around the size of the site, the amount of custom design, and the editorial workflow required.

## Starter

- Best for a single landing page or small brochure site
- Astro implementation on a lightweight content model
- Deployment setup and basic SEO configuration

## Growth

- Best for multi-page marketing sites
- Shared page templates and reusable content sections
- Blog setup, navigation structure, and CMS editing workflow

## Ongoing

- Best for teams publishing regularly
- Iterative landing pages and sub-pages
- Content model extensions and deployment support

For custom work, use the contact page to discuss scope and publishing needs.
